Spaghetti Bolognese
INGREDIENTS

3 can of Tomato Sauce
1 clove of Garlic
1 whole White Onion
1 pack of Ground Beef
1 pack of Spaghetti
Red Pepper Flakes

 RECIPE

1. Chop white onions finely
2. Grill Ground Beef, Garlic and Onions
3. When cooked, add tomato sauce
4. Boil water, add spaghetti
5. Add pasta water to the sauce
6. Put it all in a bowl, sprinkle with red pepper flakes

Pasta Vodka
INGREDIENTS

1 tube of Tomato Paste
1 whole White Onion
2 cups of Heavy Cream
1 cup OF Parmesan
1 pack of Rigatoni
Red Pepper Flakes

 RECIPE

1. Chop white onions finely
2. Add tomato paste and heavy cream
3. Boil water, add spaghetti
5. Add pasta water to the sauce
6. Put it all in a bowl, sprinkle with red pepper flakes
7. Add mozzarella balls or Parmesan (optional)

Vegetable Fried Rice
INGREDIENTS

2 cups of cooked Rice
1/2 of a white onion
1/3 can of corn
1/3 can of peas
1 piece of chicken breast
1/2 green onion
1/2 cup of carrot

RECIPE

1. Chop white onions finely
2. Cook the corn, peas, white onions and carrots
3. Heat up the rice
5. Cook chicken breasts with teriyaki or soy sauce
6. Add the total in a pan and cook
7. Sprinkle with green onions
