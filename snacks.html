Guacamole
INGREDIENTS

5 Avocadoes
1 Big Tomato
1/2 Red Onion
Cilantro
Lime/Lemon Juice
Tortilla Chips

 RECIPE

1. Mash the avocadoes
2. Chop red onions finely
3. Chop tomato finely
4. Chop cilantro finely
5. Mix Everything
6. Add lime/lemon juice (optional)
7. Enjoy with Tortilla Chips!

Hummus Platter
INGREDIENTS

1 can of chickpeas
1/4 cup tahini
1/4 cup of lemon juice
1 garlic clove
2 tbspn olive oil
1/2 tspn Paprika/Cumin

 RECIPE

1. Combine tahini and lemon juice
2. Mix chickpeas with step 1
3. Blend the mix
4. Add the garlic, olive oil and spices
5. Blend the mix again
6. Enjoy with vegetables (carrots, peppers, cucumbers)
and pita chips!
