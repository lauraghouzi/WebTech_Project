No Bake Peanut Butter Bar
INGREDIENTS

1 cup unsalted butter (melted)
2 cups of Graham Crackers
2 cups of powdered sugar
1 1/2 cups of peanut butter
1/4 cup of peanut butter (topping)
10 oz of semi-sweet chocolate (topping)

 RECIPE

1. Mix butter, crackers, sugar, peanut butter
2. Chill in the fridge for an hour
3. Melt chocolate and peanut butter
4. Spread over the top
5. Chill in fridge
6. Enjoy!

Levain Bakery Cookies
INGREDIENTS

1 cup butter
3/4 cup of brown sugar
1/2 cup of sugar
2 egg
4 cups of flour
1 tspn baking powder
1 tspn baking soda
2 cups semi sweet chocolate chips
 RECIPE

1. Mix butter, sugar and eggs in a bowl
2. Add flour
3. Add chocolate chips
4. Make dough into balls
5. Put on a baking sheet
6. Oven: 400 degrees & Time: 10min

Lemon Meringue Pie
INGREDIENTS

Homemade Pie Crust
5 egg yolks
1/3 cup of water
1 cup sugar
1 cup of fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup of lemon zest
2 tbspn butter
5 large egg whites (Meringue)
1/2 cup of sugar(Meringue)
 RECIPE

1. Put homemade pie crust in a pan
2. Mix egg yolks, sugar, water, lemon juice and zest
3. Put this mix in a pan, low heat
4. Add butter
5. Poor on pie crust
6. In another bowl, mix egg whites and sugar
7. Add meringue on top of pie
8. ENJOY!
